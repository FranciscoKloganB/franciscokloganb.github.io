About Me

  Francisco Barros, 29 years old


I am currently due to deliver my master's dissertation by the end of the current year. I study in Instituto Superior Técnico, where I learned a few programming languages, technologies, and methodologies. In an extracurricular context, I undertook an internship at Talkdesk, and I'm currently developing my photography portfolio/online shop using the Django and Bulma Frameworks. I must say it's looking great. Until then, you can see some of my enthusiast photography work on my temporary SmugMug website. Before accomplishing my dream of pursuing a software engineering career, I completed a bachelor degree in Produção Alimentar e Restauração, which roughly translates to something like, Cookery and Food Management and Security, i.e., a degree in hospitality/tourism fields. I was a pastry intern at the Ritz Lisbon, worked a few more months as a pastry man at Pastelaria Ribeiro, and then accepted a job as deputy chief of operations the famous hamburger chain, where I managed H3 Cascais for two-years. In my free time, I play tennis, frequently hit the gym, play video-games, and the physical version of Magic: The Gathering. I want to learn more about coffee, and I like to think that this is the next thing I will get into as soon as my photography platform is complete.
